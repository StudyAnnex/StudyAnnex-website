    • My Teaching Philosophy



    Claire's teaching philosophy is rooted in the belief that diversity should be celebrated and that every learner deserves to be prioritized in the classroom. She understands the importance of creating an inclusive and supportive learning environment where all students feel valued and empowered to reach their full potential. Claire is dedicated to fostering a sense of community and respect among her students, encouraging them to embrace their differences and learn from one another. My teaching philosophy centers on three core principles:


  • Active Learning: Students learn best through hands-on experiences and real-world applications
  • Differentiated Instruction: Each student deserves personalized attention and support
  • Growth Mindset: Mistakes are opportunities for learning and growth
