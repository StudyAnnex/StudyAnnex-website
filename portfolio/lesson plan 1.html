Fractions

Grade 4 Mathematics Lesson Plan

Duration: 60 minutes
Grade Level: 4th Grade
Subject: Mathematics

Learning Objectives

  • Apply fraction concepts to real-world measurement scenarios
  • Scale recipes using fraction multiplication
  • Demonstrate understanding using digital fraction tools

Materials Needed

Physical Materials

  • Measuring cups and spoons
  • Simple recipe cards
  • Ingredients for demonstration
  • Student worksheets

Digital Resources

  • Digital tablets/computers
  • Fraction visualization apps
  • Exit ticket forms

Lesson Structure

I. Opening (10 minutes)

A. Engagement Activity

  • Display measuring cup demonstrations
  • Class discussion on real-world applications
  • Create fraction use anchor chart

B. Learning Target Review

  • Share learning objectives
  • Students record familiar fractions

II. Direct Instruction (15 minutes)

A. Measurement Station Demo

  • Proper tool usage demonstration
  • Digital tool introduction
  • Practice examples

III. Guided Practice (20 minutes)

Station Rotations:

  1. Hands-on Measurement
  2. Recipe Scaling
  3. Digital Practice

IV. Independent Practice (10 minutes)

  • Kitchen Challenge worksheet
  • Extension activities

V. Closure (5 minutes)

  • Exit ticket completion
  • Learning reflection

Differentiation Strategies

Advanced Learners

  • Complex recipe calculations
  • Multiple batch scaling
  • Peer teaching opportunities

Support Strategies

  • Visual fraction aids
  • Partner work
  • Step-by-step guides

Preparation Checklist

  • Print recipe cards and worksheets
  • Set up measuring stations
  • Test digital applications
  • Create student groups
  • Prepare demonstration materials
  • Charge digital devices
  • Create visual aids

Assessment

  • Station rotation observations
  • Exit ticket responses
  • Worksheet completion accuracy
  • Student participation and engagement
